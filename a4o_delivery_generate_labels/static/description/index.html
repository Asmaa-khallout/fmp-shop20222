Module Delivery Generate Labels

Generate label(s) on selected picking(s) and merge it all in one PDF

1. In your inventory settings if you check the "Take all attachments of picking(s)" checkbox, you will download all the attachments of the selected picking(s). If you don't check that one, you will download only attachments with Label/Etiquette in the attachments names.

Then go to your inventory overview for select the picking(s) you want to generate label(s)
1. Select the pickings of your choice
2. Click on action button > Generate delivery label
All the labels or attachments (if the checkbox is checked) will be generate and downloaded directly, locally on your computer

You can check on the attachments if the label was generated. You will find them in all the pickings attachments (if already generated).

If you need more labels, you can go on the picking for who you want a new label.
1. Click on "Print a new label" for generate a new label for this picking.

Then you can find your new label(s) on your picking attachments.

Nota bene : When you will generate all the labels on your PDF, if the picking have several labels, they will be all merge on your PDF.
